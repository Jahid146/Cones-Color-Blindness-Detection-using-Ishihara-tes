About Us

Welcome to Cones Color Blindness Detection!

At Cones, we are dedicated to helping people discover more about their color vision and potential color blindness. Our mission is to provide accurate and user-friendly color vision tests that are accessible to everyone.

Our team of experts in the field of color vision and technology has developed advanced tests that allow users to easily assess their color perception. Whether you are curious about your color vision or looking for information to assist someone who might have color vision difficulties, we're here to help.

Cones was founded with the vision of making color blindness testing and awareness an integral part of individual well-being. We aim to provide valuable insights into color vision that can impact various aspects of daily life, from education and career choices to artistic pursuits.

Feel free to explore our color vision tests and resources. If you have any questions or feedback, don't hesitate to reach out to our team. Thank you for being a part of the Cones community!
